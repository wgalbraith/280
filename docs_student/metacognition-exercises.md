<h1 id="midterm-preparation">6.1 Midterm Preparation</h1>
<p>Please answer the following questions as you study for the exam:</p>
<ul>
<li>After the review, what learning objectives do you still want to work
on?</li>
<li>How will you practice these?</li>

</ul>
<h1 id="survey-questions">7 Survey Questions</h1>
<p>Please rate your agreement with the following statement with 1 for
strongly disagree and 5 for strongly agree.</p>
<ul>
<li>I am confident in my ability to apply mathematics to real-world
problems.</li>
<li>I am confident in my ability to use a calculator for math
problems.</li>
<li>I am confident in my ability to learn more mathematics in the
future.</li>
<li>Relative to the start of the class, do you have more confidence when
working on mathematics now?</li>
<li>Relative to the start of the class, do you have more perseverance
when working on mathematics?</li>
</ul>
