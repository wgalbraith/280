<h1 id="gep-280-assessment-instructions">GEP 280 Assessment Instructions</h1>
<h2 id="assessment-requirements">Assessment Requirements</h2>
<p>Your screencast narration should be 30-60 seconds in length and address the following elements for full credit</p>
<ul>
<li>Your thought process for the problem should be evident in the narration</li>
<li>Your narration must demonstrate your understanding of the concepts and computation</li>
<li>Your work on the page must be complete and accurate</li>
<li>Your work must adhere to conventions of quantitative communication
<ul>
<li>Must have a reasonable number of significant figures</li>
<li>Must use scientific notation when appropriate</li>
</ul></li>
</ul>
<p>If you do not get credit on your first attempt, you may repeat for credit, with a small points deduction. Your repeat work should also address the following elements for full credit</p>
<ul>
<li>Narration should focus on the change in your work since the previous attempt</li>
<li>Narration should mention the change in your thinking or mental models since the previous attempt.</li>
</ul>
<h2 id="assessment-assignment-tips">Assessment Assignment Tips</h2>
<p>Here is a suggested workflow to create your video assignments for these. Let me know if you find apps or ideas that could help others.</p>
<ul>
<li>Take a photo or scan of your assignment, ensure it is legible.</li>
<li>Use Zoom (or other screencasting software) to create a video focused on the window with the scan.</li>
</ul>
<h1 id="ssu-estimation">1 SSU Estimation</h1>
<p>Come up with an estimate that involves all the students at SSU and describe a simple model to calculate it. Show how you computed your estimate and provide your estimate.</p>
