<h1 id="populations-and-samples">16 Populations and Samples</h1>
<p>Using the CES data columns, propose an “effect” that you’d like to test using a population and a sample.</p>
<p>Write a short essay (150 words) addressing</p>
<ul>
<li>The overall question you are trying to answer.</li>
<li>Why taking a sample of the population will help answer the question.</li>
<li>How you’ll select your sample. Be specific about the columns and numbers you’ll use.</li>
<li>How you’ll determine if difference between the mean of your sample and the overall mean is likely to have been caused by random variation</li>
</ul>
<p>To receive full credit</p>
<ul>
<li>meet the word length</li>
<li>demonstrate understanding of important concepts including standard error, population, and sample</li>
<li>demonstrate engagement with the question</li>
</ul>
