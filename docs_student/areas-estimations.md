## Areas
- Notice that these all involve the length and the width and a factor

| shape     |  area             |
|-----------|-------------------|
| rectangle | $$l \cdot w$$       |
| circle    | $$0.79\ l \cdot w$$ |
| ellipse   | $$0.79\ l \cdot w$$ |

## Area
- Has dimension of length squared
